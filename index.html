Wonderland Indonesia

Indonesia is known by its official name, the Republic of Indonesia, or more fully, the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia. It is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia that crosses the equator and is situated between the Asian mainland and Oceania, making it known as a transcontinental country. It is also located between the Pacific Ocean and the Indian Ocean.

Indonesia is the 14th largest country in the world by land area and the largest archipelagic nation, covering an area of 1,904,569 square kilometers. It is also the sixth country in the world with the highest number of islands, totaling 17,504 islands.

What?

Indonesia boasts breathtaking natural beauty with its diverse landscapes.

Where?

This stunning beauty can be found throughout the Indonesian archipelago.

When?

Indonesia's beauty is on display year-round, thanks to its tropical climate.

Indonesia's beauty is a result of its unique geographical features.
